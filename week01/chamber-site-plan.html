Site Plan for La Plata Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

La Plata Chamber of Commerce

The site name was selected because clearly identifies the content of the website .

Site Purpose

Our chamber of commerce website serves the following purposes:

  • Our Chamber of Commerce website serves as the central hub for our business community. We provide a platform for local businesses to connect, grow, and thrive .
  • You can explore local businesses, stay informed about upcoming events, and collaborate with like-minded professionals to build a prosperous and welcoming community

Scenarios

Our website will address the following scenarios for our target audience:

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Chosen colors to our website:

Primary Color: #254E70
Secondary Color: #333

Primary color will be used for headings, while the secondary color will be used for accents and buttons.

Typography

Fonts for the website:

Heading Font: ['Roboto']
Body Font: ['sans-serif']

Heading font will be used for headings, and the body font will be used for the main content.
