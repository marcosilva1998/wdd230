Site Plan for La Plata Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

La Plata Chamber of Commerce

The site name was selected because clearly identifies the content of the website .

Site Purpose

The website serves the following purposes:

  • The Chamber of Commerce website serves as the central hub for our business community. We provide a platform for local businesses to connect, grow, and thrive .
  • You can explore local businesses, stay informed about upcoming events, and collaborate with like-minded professionals to build a prosperous and welcoming community

Scenarios

The website will address the following scenarios for the target audience:

  1. How can the chamber of commerce help me start and grow my new business?
  2. What local events, festivals, and family-friendly activities are scheduled in the coming months within the chamber's area?
  3. What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber of commerce, and what is the cost associated with different membership tiers?

Color Schema

Chosen colors to the website:

Primary Color: #254E70
Secondary Color: #333

Primary color will be used for headings, te secondaty will used for 'p>' elements .

Typography

Fonts for the website:

Heading Font: ['Roboto','Oswald']
Body Font: ['sans-serif' ,'Open Sans'']

Heading font will be used for headings, and the body font will be used for the content in 'p' elements.
